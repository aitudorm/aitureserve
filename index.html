FAQ

The concept of co-working (co-working) appeared in 2005. Of course, it began to appear in our country much later. The essence of coworking is that a person working on a freelance basis, a novice entrepreneur or another "freelance artist", chooses for his work not a house or cafe, but an office space with the necessary amenities, in which everyone can work.

There are several factors here. First, it's the price. When renting an office, you pay not only for square meters, but also for utilities. In addition, you often have to pay quarterly, and this is not a small amount. With us, everything is much simpler. You pay not for meters, but for the workplace.

We have video surveillance cameras in all the main rooms, as well as security. Many of our residents settle into their jobs and feel at home. And you can, too. During the entire time of the coworking, not a single case of theft was registered, but still do not push your luck, so we do not recommend leaving money and jewelry unattended.
